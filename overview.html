course overview¬∂

As mentioned on the Welcome page, this course will be focus on re-executable publications and cover various important aspects from an introduction to the problem and motivation to adapt neuroimaging research workflows accordingly to the utilization of basic and advanced tools/resources. In general, the idea is to do a split between first, the basics of how to write a re-executable publication and second, providing first hands-on experience based on examples. Regarding the first, we will try to provide attendees with a brief overview of central aspects, as well as important issues and concerning the second, work through an example workflow from beginning to end together. Thus, we will go through and practice the use of respective software, tools and resources. We will further explain both aspects, as well as the setup, etc. below. For a precise outline of this course, please consult the respective page.
